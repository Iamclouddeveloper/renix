Derby Hindu Temple (Geeta Bhavan) – Digital Modernisation & Cloud-Based Infrastructure

This case study highlights the digital transformation roadmap developed by RENIX (UK) for Derby Hindu Temple (Geeta Bhavan). The goal was to introduce modern cloud-based systems, streamline document management, and create immersive digital experiences for visitors.

Official Website: https://www.derbyhindutemple.com

Project Overview

As part of their digital progression, Derby Hindu Temple required a structured digital foundation to manage data efficiently, improve transparency, and make temple information accessible in a modern and interactive format. RENIX (UK) introduced a scalable system focusing on cloud solutions, digital archiving, and virtual temple experiences.

Key Digital Enhancements

  • Cloud Data Management (Google Drive Integration): A unified cloud storage structure to organise documents, event files, financial summaries, meeting notes, and archive data. The system enables restricted access sharing, version control, and secure digital backups.
  • Digital Document Repository: Centralised access to forms, policies, schedules, and administrative files. The repository provides easy retrieval, improved transparency, and secure long-term storage.
  • 3D Temple Virtual View: An interactive 3D model of the temple that allows visitors to explore key areas digitally. This feature supports remote viewing, virtual walkthroughs, and accessibility for users unable to visit physically.
  • Online Booking & Hall Management (Future Upgrade): A planned system for managing hall reservations, event requests, and booking confirmations through automated workflows and calendar-based scheduling.
  • Digital Communication System: Cloud-based announcements, event updates, and notifications for users through integrated email and website CMS updates.
  • Facebook Integration Tool (New Feature): Seamless Facebook API integration enabling:
    • Automatic posting of temple updates to the Facebook page
    • Real-time syncing of events and announcements
    • Embedded Facebook feed directly on the website
    • Increased community engagement through social media automation

Outcome & Forward Strategy

These digital upgrades establish a solid foundation for Derby Hindu Temple’s long-term digital presence. Cloud organisation, structured data access, and interactive experiences help support administrative efficiency and improve public engagement. Future developments include mobile app integration, expanded virtual tours, and automated event workflows.

Digital solution designed and maintained by RENIX (UK) Limited
