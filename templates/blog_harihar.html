Welcome to the Official Website of Acharya Harihar Rajan Iyengar

Acharya Harihar Rajan Iyengar is a Vedic priest and spiritual guide from Bangalore, brings decades of knowledge in Sanatan Dharma to families across the UAE. Fluent in English, Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, and Kannada, he performs traditional rituals and consultations with authenticity, precision, and heartfelt devotion.

Biography

Born into a lineage of Vedic scholars, Acharya Harihar Rajan Iyengar exhibited a profound inclination towards spiritual studies from an early age. Under the tutelage of esteemed gurus, he mastered the intricacies of the Vedas, Upanishads, and other sacred scriptures. His teachings emphasise the practical application of ancient wisdom in contemporary life, aiming to bridge the gap between tradition and modernity.

Teachings and Philosophy

  • Vedic Discourses: In-depth explorations of Vedic literature, elucidating their relevance in today's world.
  • Spiritual Workshops: Interactive sessions focusing on meditation, ethical living, and self-realisation.
  • Cultural Preservation: Initiatives aimed at preserving and promoting traditional arts, rituals, and festivals.
  • Community Engagement: Organising events and seminars to foster communal harmony and spiritual awareness.

Services Offered

  • Spiritual Consultations: Personalised astrological and dharmic guidance for all phases of life.
  • Religious Ceremonies: Grihapravesham, Satyanarayan Puja, Ganapathi Homam, Shraddha rituals, and more.
  • Life Events: Naming ceremonies (Namakarana), Thread ceremonies (Upanayanam), Weddings, and Baby Showers.
  • Katha & Storytelling: Engaging and educational narrations from Ramayana, Mahabharata, and Puranas.
