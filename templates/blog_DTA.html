Derby Telugu Association

Welcome to the official website of the Derby Telugu Association a vibrant and growing community dedicated to promoting Telugu culture, language, traditions, and unity among the Telugu-speaking families residing in and around Derby, UK.

Our Mission

The Derby Telugu Association (DTA) was established to preserve and celebrate the rich heritage of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana through festivals, cultural programs, spiritual gatherings, and social initiatives. We aim to build strong connections among Telugu families and provide a platform for children and adults alike to stay connected with their roots.

What We Do

  • Festivals & Celebrations: We organise traditional Telugu events such as Sankranti, Ugadi, Dasara, and Diwali, bringing our community together in joyous celebration.
  • Cultural Events: From music and dance performances to storytelling and drama, we provide a stage for cultural expression and youth engagement.
  • Community Support: We offer help and guidance to new families settling in Derby, including accommodation, school enrollment, and social networking.
  • Spiritual Gatherings: Regular satsangs, bhajans, and pujas help us stay spiritually connected and grounded in our values.
  • Educational Activities: We encourage Telugu language learning and moral storytelling sessions for children.

Get Involved

We warmly welcome volunteers, sponsors, and community members who wish to contribute their time, talents, or resources. Whether you’re a performer, organiser, teacher, or supporter there’s always a place for you in our family.
