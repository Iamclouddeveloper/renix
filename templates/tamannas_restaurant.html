This case study highlights how Tamannas Restaurant modernised its dine-in operations through a fully integrated digital ecosystem designed by RENIX (UK). The objective was to improve order accuracy, enhance customer throughput, and streamline backend workflows using smart restaurant technology.

Project Overview

Tamannas Restaurant required a unified system capable of managing high-volume orders, reducing manual dependencies, and providing real-time visibility across billing, kitchen operations, and customer notifications. RENIX (UK) implemented a tailored digital solution that connected EPOS, kitchen operations, and customer engagement tools into one seamless platform.

Operational Goals

  • Reduce order processing time and manual errors.
  • Improve communication between service staff and kitchen teams.
  • Enable real-time customer updates for a transparent dining experience.
  • Create a scalable system that supports future expansion.

Digital Solution Implemented by RENIX (UK)

The technology foundation of Tamannas was built using a modular architecture, allowing the restaurant to manage complex workflows efficiently while maintaining flexibility for future upgrades.

Key Functional Components

  • EPOS System: A high-performance billing platform enabling order tracking, discount automation, and instantaneous receipt generation.
  • Real-Time Notification System: Automated alerts for:
    • Order Accepted
    • Order in Preparation
    • Order Ready to Serve
    • Payment Updates
  • Billing & Invoice Engine: Generates accurate invoices with applied discounts, itemised summaries, and table-wise reporting.
  • Queue & Table Management: Structured flow for handling walk-ins, table allocation, waiting lists, and occupancy status.
  • Kitchen Display System (KDS): Live order feed featuring priorities, timestamps, and preparation status, improving kitchen efficiency.

Outcome & Impact

The digital transformation led to measurable improvements in turnaround time, order accuracy, and overall customer experience. Staff coordination became more efficient, and management gained real-time operational insights, enabling data-driven decision-making.
