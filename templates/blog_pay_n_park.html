Pay N Park – Smart Parking Platform for Commercial & Public Users

Pay N Park is an intelligent parking management platform engineered by RENIX (UK) Limited. Built for both commercial operators and everyday motorists, the platform simplifies parking access through real-time availability, smart reservations, and seamless cashless payments.

Project Overview

The Pay N Park system was designed to reduce congestion, improve parking turnover, and eliminate manual ticketing processes. Users can locate, reserve, and pay for parking spaces effortlessly, while commercial operators benefit from automated workflows and live occupancy controls.

  • Real-time visibility of available parking spaces
  • Advance booking with fully automated payments
  • GPS navigation to guide users directly to their parking slot
  • Personalised user experience with saved locations & preferences
