FlyerPro – Smart Leaflet Distribution

FlyerPro is a smart, location-based leaflet distribution platform developed by RENIX (UK) Limited. Designed for modern marketing needs, it simplifies flyer handling, GPS-tracked deliveries, and real-time campaign monitoring all through a single, intuitive dashboard.

What is FlyerPro?

FlyerPro empowers businesses, marketers, and distribution teams to streamline the entire flyer delivery process. From design to doorstep, the platform offers visibility, automation, and control over every step of the campaign, backed by real-time tracking and analytics.

Official Website: https://flyerpro.co.uk/

Key Features

  • Real-Time GPS Tracking:

    Track flyer deliveries live on a map using Leaflet.js and WebSocket technology. Monitor team movement, coverage history, and check-ins as they happen.

  • Team and Task Management:

    Assign postcodes and delivery areas, monitor team performance, and organise schedules from a user-friendly admin panel.

  • Smart Flyer Handling:

    Prevent duplicates and missed deliveries using automated route logging, delivery status checks, and historical data.

  • End-to-End Service:

    From flyer design and print to delivery and tracking FlyerPro handles every stage with reliability and style.

Technology Stack Behind FlyerPro

FlyerPro is built using modern, scalable tools to ensure a smooth and secure experience:

  • Flask (Python) backend for handling tasks and API logic
  • SQL database to manage users, tasks, addresses, and tracking history
  • Leaflet.js for real-time map-based GPS tracking
  • JavaScript + Bootstrap frontend for responsive UI
  • AWS hosting and storage for scalability and security

Why FlyerPro Stands Out

  • Transparency: Live tracking ensures you always know where your flyers are going.
  • Simplicity: Clean interface and minimal training required for teams to get started.
  • Control: Admins can monitor, assign, and analyse campaigns from one place.
