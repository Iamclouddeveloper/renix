FlyerPro – Smart Leaflet Distribution

FlyerPro is a location-intelligent leaflet distribution platform developed by RENIX (UK) Limited. It centralises flyer handling, live delivery tracking, and campaign analytics in one intuitive dashboard—ideal for businesses seeking accuracy, transparency, and performance-driven marketing.

What is FlyerPro?

FlyerPro is a smart, GPS-enabled leaflet distribution system designed to help teams plan, deliver, and monitor campaigns with complete clarity. From assigning postcodes to logging each delivery, all activity is recorded in real time for precise management and reporting.

  • End-to-end workflow: Design, Print, Deliver
  • Live GPS tracking with route history and checkpoints
  • Smart delivery logging to prevent duplicates or missed addresses
  • Team scheduling and performance monitoring built in
